Everybody need somebody

Sometimes to love

Or to be loved by

Sometimes to take care

Or to be taken care by

Or in the end

Just hurt or Get hurt by

I guess it's all in the genes

We all need someone

Either we have love to share or pain

which depends on

If we plan to hurt or cure them
